Clock Geometry
===============

Control the overall size and tick mark appearance of the clock face.

* ``outerRel`` (0.95) - Clock outer radius relative to screen height
* ``minorInnerRel`` (0.816) - Inner radius for minor tick marks
* ``majorInnerRel`` (0.774) - Inner radius for major tick marks
* ``minorOuterRel`` (0.955) - Outer radius for minor tick marks
* ``minorWidthRel`` (0.009) - Width of minor tick marks
* ``majorWidthRel`` (0.026) - Width of major tick marks
* ``centerCircleRel`` (0.038) - Radius of center circle
